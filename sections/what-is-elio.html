What is ELIO

One Board.
Every Connection.

ELIO is a Nordic nRF52-based physical computing platform designed for makers, educators, and AI developers. Connect motors, sensors, and cameras — then control everything from blocks, Python, Arduino, or an AI agent.

Open Hardware AI-Native BLE + LoRa
elio-specs.json
MCU Nordic nRF52 (BLE built-in)
DC Motors ×2 (PWM speed control)
Servo ×2 (−90° ~ +90°)
I/O Ports ×4 (LED / sensor)

Comms BLE / LoRa / USB Serial
Battery 3V – 9V
